## MODULE USAGE RULES (consultant only; stripped from client version)

1. **This is a section set, not a standalone IDR.** In a multi-module engagement, these items merge into one combined client-facing request list under the D2 cover letter, sequenced after or alongside the SUT sections. The coordinator receives one list, one shared folder structure, one response deadline, and one checkpoint call, regardless of how many modules the engagement covers.
2. **All D2 internal usage rules apply unmodified:** the two-version rule, the status values and their triggers, the register linkage rule (Does Not Exist and Declined become candidate findings sourced to the IDR ID), the checkpoint call, and the cross-reference rule.
3. **Held by Provider means held by the preparer.** The status value stays "Held by Provider" so the coordinator sees one status vocabulary across a combined engagement; for this module's items, the provider will most often be the external return preparer or provision assistance firm. The treatment is unchanged from D2: legitimate answer, item routes to the provider-held items list carried into the Q-EA interview, a pattern of preparer-held records is evaluated as a candidate Dimension 8 observation, and a held item the preparer cannot produce becomes a finding sourced to both. Client records held only by the preparer (elections history, tax basis data, e-file confirmations) are a recurring transition finding at this client profile; watch for the pattern.
4. **Shared items are not re-requested.** The following D2 items serve this module and are cross-referenced rather than duplicated. When running a federal-only engagement without the SUT module, lift these into the combined list under their original IDs:
   - IDR-D1-01 (finance org chart), IDR-D1-02 (delegation of authority matrix), IDR-D1-05 (board and sponsor reporting), IDR-D1-09 (outside advisor list)
   - IDR-D2-03 (chart of accounts extract, tax accounts), IDR-D2-08 (records retention policy)
   - IDR-D3-06 (legal entity list)
   - IDR-D5-01 through IDR-D5-06 (talent items; this module adds federal-specific overlays below rather than repeating them)
   - IDR-D6-08 (acquisition tax due diligence reports)
   - IDR-D7-05 (data room index from the last capital event)
5. **Customization per engagement.** Delete items clearly inapplicable before sending. The master is a ceiling, not a script.
